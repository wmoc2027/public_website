{{< collapse id="preamble" class="p-3 border rounded" >}}

In May 2027, the World Masters Games (WMG), an international competition for lifelong sports, will be held in the Kansai region, where anyone over the age of 30[^fn1] can participate. There are 35 sports and 59 events as official competitions, and the orienteering competition is one of them, but it is also held as the World Masters Orienteering Championships (WMOC).

[^fn1]: Orienteering competitions are 35 years old and above

WMOC2027 is expected to attract more than 1,000 participants from overseas as the first tournament in Asia, and it is expected to produce champions from Japan.

There are sprint events held in urban areas and parks, and forest events held in forests and forests, and are held in Yabu City, Kamigawa Town, and Kami Town, Hyogo Prefecture.

One year before the tournament, in order to smoothly operate the world-class tournament and enjoy it for those who participate, we will hold a rehearsal competition aimed at simulating the operation together with the executive committee of the host municipality (Yabu City, Kamigawa Town, and Kami Town).

We will operate the WMG/WMOC2027 tournament with the assumption that it will be a WMG tournament.
