    {{< card title="WMG Registration / WMG Masters Village" icon="fas 1" >}}
WMG2027 Participant accreditation will be online. Materials and transport passes for all WMG participants will be available to pick up at the Masters Village in each WMG City. 
> [!NOTE]
> The locations of the Masters Village are currently not finally decided.
    {{< /card >}}
    {{< card title="Get race number card and other tools / WMOC Event Center" icon="fas 2" >}}
The WMOC Event Centre is at [the Kasumi B&G Marine Centre](https://maps.app.goo.gl/DgHjt1WfHyfLfLCx5), Kami Town, Hyogo.
Registration (race number etc. collection) for WMOC orienteers will be at the Event Centre on 20 and 21 May 2027. There will also be an Information Desk at each orienteering race venue, where registration can also be made.
    {{< /card >}}
    {{< card title="Join your registerd races" icon="fas 3" >}}
Once you have received your number card and other necessary documents at the event center, the race will begin at the start time published on this website. Please make sure to arrive at the starting area with plenty of time to spare and to have a sufficient warm-up.
    {{< /card >}}
